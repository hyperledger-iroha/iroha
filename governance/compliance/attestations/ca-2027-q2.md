# SoraNet GAR Attestation — Canada (2027-Q2)

- **Review date:** 2027-03-18
- **Reviewed by:** SoraNet Legal & Compliance Desk (Privacy + Telecom working group)
- **Decision:** Direct-only transport required for Canadian endpoints while privacy redaction and lawful-access assurances are evaluated.
- **Rationale:** PIPEDA/CPPA obligations and provincial data export controls require that operators can demonstrate deterministic path inspection and retention controls; anonymous relays hinder compliance verification and breach notification timelines.
- **Scope:** Applies to SoraFS gateway fetch/upload traffic, relay incentives, DA ingestion/fetches, and Torii `/v1/stream` backhaul when any party is Canada-based. Cross-border flows involving Canadian endpoints must also use direct-only hops.
- **Conditions/expiry:** Re-evaluate by 2027-09-30, contingent on privacy redaction audit and regulator feedback.
- **Operational requirements:**
  - Publish the attestation digest in `compliance.attestations` under `jurisdiction: "CA"` and keep `jurisdiction_opt_outs` synced.
  - Store the signed PDF memo (`ca-2027-q2.pdf`) in the governance archive; automation relies on the Blake2b-256 digest of this Markdown companion for rollouts.
  - Record activation in the local GAR logbook with timestamp, tag, and approver list.
- **Contacts:** `governance@soranet.org`, `legal@soranet.org`

The canonical digest for this attestation (Blake2b-256, uppercase hex) is recorded in the jurisdictional review log.
