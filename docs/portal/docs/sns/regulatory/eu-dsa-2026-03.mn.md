## 1. Intake Summary

- **Bulletin:** European Commission “DNS-based hosting services – transparency briefing” (Ref. DSA/2026/0311).
- **Key requirements:** retain resolver contact metadata for 12 months, publish abuse-handling transparency addendum, and ensure EU-resident dispute reviewers for consumer complaints.
- **Submission date:** 2026‑03‑12.
- **Deadline:** 2026‑04‑15 (compliance declaration due to DG CONNECT).
